Our Story

From a Vision to an Enterprise Platform

Golden Key Techy was founded with a clear purpose: to solve the real operational pain points that education and healthcare institutions face daily — through well-designed, powerful software.

Starting with a single school ERP deployment, we've grown to serve 50+ clients across 10+ industries, building a reputation for reliable delivery, domain expertise, and exceptional support.

Today, our flagship platforms GK EduERP and GK HealthERP are trusted by schools, colleges, hospitals, and diagnostic centres — with a pipeline of enterprise and government clients in active discussions.
